Own Kenya is an initiative inspired by a need for Kenyan citizens to focus on developing their country economically and along cultural basis.

The main objective is to provide an online platform where information about major Kenyan projects and developments in public and private sector is posted. This will in turn attract interest of people to follow and shape the way for these developments.

The Own Kenya platform will integrate with other social networking platforms such as Facebook, Google+, Twitter, LinkedIn.

The platform shall be accessible via mobile phone applications and desktop web, that allows members connect and share updates about regional as well as national developments.
